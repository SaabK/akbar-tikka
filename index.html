Our Story

A Gujranwala Favourite, One Skewer at a Time

Akbar Tikka Shop started as a single charcoal grill on a busy Gujranwala street corner, run on nothing but a family recipe and an open flame. Years later, the smoke still rises every evening, the marinade is still made by hand, and the same promise stands: every skewer is grilled fresh, never reheated, never rushed.

What began as a small stall is now a name regulars bring out-of-town guests to try — because in Gujranwala, a city famous for its food, this is still the corner everyone points to.

  • 🔥 Charcoal-grilled, never microwaved
  • 🥩 Fresh meat sourced daily
  • 👨‍👩‍👧 Family-run, family-friendly seating
  • ⭐ 12+ years of local reputation
